Rouse

Keep your Mac awake for AI agents, long downloads, exports, and presentations with timed sessions, display sleep control, and battery-aware rules, then control sessions from the menu bar, Dashboard, Terminal, or scripts.

Rouse is available on the Mac App Store. Version 1.7 adds the official rouse CLI for command line and automation workflows.
