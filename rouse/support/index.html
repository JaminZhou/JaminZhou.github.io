Rouse Support

Rouse gives you a calm dashboard for wake sessions, display sleep control, and battery-aware rules, with a menu bar shortcut always available when you just need a quick toggle.

Need help? Contact me@jaminzhou.com.

Email support

What to include

  • Your Rouse version and build number
  • Your macOS version
  • A short description of what you expected and what happened instead
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible
  • A screenshot if it helps explain the problem

Common topics

  • Timed sessions ending earlier or later than expected
  • Display sleep behavior
  • Battery protection settings
  • Dashboard opening, reopen behavior, or launch at login
  • Questions about App Store releases or updates
Support requests are answered in English. Please avoid sending passwords, account secrets, or other sensitive personal information.
