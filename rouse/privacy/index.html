Rouse Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Rouse for macOS, including the Mac App Store version.

Summary

Rouse works locally on your Mac. The app gives you quick menu bar controls and a fuller dashboard without requiring an account, ads, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Information Rouse does not collect

  • No account information
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics data
  • No location data
  • No contact list, photo library, microphone, or camera data

Information stored locally on your device

Rouse stores app preferences locally on your Mac so the app can work as expected. Depending on the features you use, this may include:

  • Your preferred session duration
  • Whether display sleep prevention is enabled
  • Battery protection settings and threshold
  • Launch at login preference

This information stays on your device and is not sent to the developer by the app.

Network activity

The Mac App Store version of Rouse works locally for its core wake-control features. It does not require sign-in or a remote account to use the menu bar controls, open the dashboard, start sessions, or manage rules.

Support requests

If you contact support by email, the information you choose to send, such as your email address and the contents of your message, will be used only to respond to your request and improve support quality.

Third-party services

If you use a third-party email provider or other external service to contact support, your information may also be subject to that service's own terms and privacy practices.

Children's privacy

Rouse is not directed to children, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact me@jaminzhou.com.
